About

Lumière Glow Serum pairs a stable 10% vitamin C derivative with 4% niacinamide and Centella Asiatica to fade dark spots, even skin tone and restore a quiet, lit-from-within radiance — without the sting of pure ascorbic acid.

Why it works

  • 10% Tetrahexyldecyl Ascorbate — gentle, oil-soluble vitamin C that brightens without irritation.
  • 4% Niacinamide — softens the look of pores and supports the skin barrier.
  • Centella Asiatica & Ceramide NP — calm redness and lock in moisture.
  • Suitable for all skin types, including sensitive and post-procedure skin.

How to use

Apply 3–4 drops to clean, dry skin every morning before moisturiser and SPF. Build up to twice daily after two weeks if your skin tolerates it well.
